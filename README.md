
## Project Overview

Book Nook is a personalized reading tracking app designed to help users build a consistent reading habit and to make reading more engaging. This app combines book tracking, recommendations, and personal reflection tools. It also offers a personal vault where readers can save book reviews and quotes, making it easy to revisit your favorite passages. 

## Features

- Customizable Bookshelf: Add books to your own bookshelf by scanning barcodes or search books by its title or author
- Personal Vault: Save reviews and your favorite quotes
- Book Recommendation: Suggests books based on user's preferences, such as genres, length, and keywords
- Habit Tracker & Stats: Keeps track of user's reading activities
